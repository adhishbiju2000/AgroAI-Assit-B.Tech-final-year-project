Our Mission

At AgroAI Assist, we aim to revolutionize agriculture by providing farmers with cutting-edge AI tools to monitor soil health, detect plant diseases, and receive real-time weather alerts. Our goal is to ensure sustainable farming practices and maximize crop yield for crops like tomatoes and potatoes, making precision agriculture accessible to farmers everywhere.

Our Journey

Project Inception

January 2024

Identified the need for AI-powered agricultural solutions after researching farmers' challenges.

AI Model Development

March 2024

Developed and trained deep learning models for disease detection using thousands of plant images.

Platform Beta Launch

June 2024

Launched beta version with core features and began testing with local farming communities.

Full Platform Release

December 2024

Released the complete platform with weather integration and mobile optimization.
